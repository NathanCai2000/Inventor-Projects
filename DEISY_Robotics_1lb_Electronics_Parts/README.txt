Design Restrictions:
- You may ONLY uses the listed and provided parts
- You are allowed to modify the FT Tire to the different existing sizes:
	a)Thickness 1/2in: 1in, 1.25in, 1.5in, 1.75in, 2in, or 2.25in
	b)Thickness 3/4in: 2.25in, 2.5in, 2.75in, or 3in
- ALL parts other parts (excluding screws, shafts, springs, standoffs and electronics) are to be made of normal PLA
- CF Nylon, TPU, PETG, PLA+, etc are banned
- Screws, shafts, standoffs, and all other non-PLA items are NOT allowed to be used for:
	a) Impactor on weapons
	b) Weights for weapons
	c) Used for armor
- All rules will follow SPARC design rules
	a) Link: http://sparc.tools/
